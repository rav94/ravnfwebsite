About Me

Hi, I’m Ravindu Nirmal Fernando, welcome to my personal website...

I am a cloud, DevSecOps, platform engineering, and engineering leadership professional currently serving as VP of Engineering at Emojot, a customer experience SaaS platform. My work sits at the intersection of product engineering, cloud architecture, secure delivery, compliance, reliability, and people leadership.

Over the years, I have led engineering teams, designed internal developer platforms, shaped cloud operating models, worked closely with customers and executives, and helped turn technical platforms into business-enabling products. I care deeply about building teams that own outcomes, platforms that reduce cognitive load, and systems that are secure, scalable, and useful in the real world.

My core areas of interest include platform engineering, DevSecOps, cloud security, SaaS architecture, engineering strategy, developer experience, compliance readiness, and building high-trust engineering organizations.
